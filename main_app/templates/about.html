About the Plant Collector

I love taking care of houseplants and watching them grow! My collection started with just one succulent, and now I have a mini jungle in my apartment.

Fun Fact: I name all my plants after characters from my favorite books.

When I'm not watering my plants or checking for new growth, I enjoy long walks through plant nurseries and botanical gardens. I'm always looking for new leafy friends to add to my collection!

This app helps me keep track of all my plant babies - their watering schedules, sunlight needs, and when I last repotted them. It's like a digital plant journal!
